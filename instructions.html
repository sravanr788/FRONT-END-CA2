Instructions:

Gameplay

Click on a tile to reveal its contents. Here's what you need to watch out for:

  • If the clicked tile is empty, your score increases. Keep uncovering those empty tiles to boost your score.
  • But, there's danger lurking. If you accidentally click on a hidden mine, it triggers a blast and increases the mine count.

Winning and Losing:

  • To win the game, your aim is to score 20 POINTS.
  • However, be cautious! If you click on 4 BOMBS it's game over, and you'll have to start again.

Timer

  • A timer will track the time it takes to complete the game. Challenge yourself to clear the grid in the fastest time possible!

Multiplayer Mode

  • In multiplayer mode, you can compete against other players in real-time. Test your skills and see who can clear the grid first or with the highest score.

Hint System

  • If you ever find yourself stuck or in need of assistance, use the hint button. It reveals a tile containing bomb. Use it wisely!

Tie condition

  • If winning condition meets the losing condition it results in a tie condition. Keep an eye on your moves!
